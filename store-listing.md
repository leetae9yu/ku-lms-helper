## Summary (short description, max 132 chars)
Extract video transcripts and quiz data from Korea University LMS with one click.

## Description
KU LMS Helper is a Chrome extension designed for Korea University students to easily extract study materials from the LMS.

**Features:**
- 📝 Extract quiz questions, answers, and explanations
- 🎥 Extract video transcripts with timestamps
- 💾 Download as TXT or JSON format
- 🌙 Dark mode support
- 🔒 Privacy-focused (all processing happens locally)

**How to use:**
1. Navigate to your quiz result page or lecture video
2. Click the extension icon
3. Click "Extract" button
4. Download your study materials

**Privacy:**
- No data sent to external servers
- No tracking or analytics
- Your login session is never stored

Perfect for exam preparation and lecture review!
